Key Features of DogArmor

Smart, personalized, and built for your dog’s unique needs.

📸 Image-Based Recognition

Upload a photo of your dog to help us better understand their body type and age — it boosts accuracy so we can recommend the most comfortable gear. A dog's anatomy is crucial when choosing and fitting a harness. A well-fitting harness should be designed to support and distribute weight evenly, minimizing pressure points and allowing for natural movement. Our image recognition ai technology will examine your dog’s anatomy and provide a harness that best fits every skeletal structure.

🐾 Behavioral Matching

Takes into account your dog’s activity level, age, and behavior to recommend gear that suits their personality and habits.

📊 Data-Driven Suggestions

Our system uses curated data to match breed, size, and budget with the best collar, harness, and leash options for them.

💰 Budget Flexibility

Whether you need something budget-friendly or top-quality, we’ll recommend the right gear for your dog.

📱 Mobile-Ready Future

We’ll be offering a mobile experience next, so you'll be able to scan, search, and shop on the go.

🔐 Privacy First

No tracking. No selling your data. Just private, personalized recommendations — and you're always in charge.
