About DogArmor

Built to help every dog find the perfect gear — intelligently, compassionately, and affordably.

Our Mission

Our focus is to make life easier for dog owners. Our AI-powered recommendations take the guesswork out of choosing the right collars, harnesses, and leashes — all based on your dog’s unique traits. No more confusion. Just gear that fits, works, and makes every walk better.

What Makes Us Different

We don’t just guess. Our system combines your dog’s photo with their breed, size, and behavior data to deliver gear recommendations that actually work — for comfort, safety, and real-life fit. Your dog isn’t a size chart. That’s why we are the first company to go beyond the basics — combining images and data to understand each pup’s unique needs. It’s how we deliver gear that actually fits, feels good, and works in real life.

From image to insight — built to understand every dog’s unique needs.

How It All Started

Interesting enough, it all started with a simple thought: “Why is it so hard to find the right gear for your dog?” To answer this, we built a solution that brings clarity and convenience, rather than confusion, to pet care.

What We Believe In

  • Technology should focus on solving real problems
  • Simple design, but smart results
  • Every dog deserves gear that fits just right
