Why Our Company Matters

At DogArmor, we believe every dog deserves gear that fits perfectly — because a happy dog means a happy owner. Our mission is to combine technology and care to make choosing dog gear simple, smart, and stress-free.

Solving Real Problems

Finding the right collar, harness, or leash can be overwhelming with so many options. We use AI and data-driven insights to cut through the noise and deliver personalized recommendations tailored to your dog's unique needs.

Built on Compassion

Our team is made up of dog lovers, data scientists, and product experts committed to improving pet owners’ lives. We understand that your dog’s comfort and safety come first.

Innovation for the Future

We’re continuously refining our AI to include more breeds, behaviors, and gear options. Our upcoming mobile app will make it even easier to get the perfect gear on the go.

Ethics & Privacy

Your data belongs to you. We never sell or share your information, and prioritize your privacy in every feature we build.
